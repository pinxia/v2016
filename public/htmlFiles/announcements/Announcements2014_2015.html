SCCS Fall 2015 First Day of School

中文

Dear SCCS Students, Teachers and Parents,

Welcome back! We trusted that you and your family had a wonderful summer and are ready for another exciting academic year at the Southern Connecticut Chinese School! This is a friendly reminder that our school will start this Sunday, September 13, 2015. We will have Chinese Language lessons this week, but enrichment lessons will start next week on September 20. In order to make things run smoothly on the first day of school, we would like to go through a brief “to-do” list:

  1. Class Registration

    As last year, our registration table is located cross hallway from the cafeteria in B Wing. For those of you who are new to our school, please ask our staff at the front entrance of Engleman Hall A Wing for direction. - If you have already mailed payment voucher and check, you can simply skip this step. Almost 200 families have registered and paid during the summer, we would like to express our deep gratitude for your generous support! - If you have registered classes online but haven’t made the payment, please print out the payment voucher and bring it with the check to the registration table; If you have an account but haven’t registered classes, please make sure to get your Family ID and Student ID numbers. It will make the registration process much easier; - If you are new to our school and haven’t set up an account, don’t worry, our staff will help you get registered.

  2. Finding Classroom

    A school-wide class list will be posted at the front entrance of Engleman Hall A Wing, and at the registration table in B Wing as well. Outside each classroom, there will be a name list of the students who are taking classes in that room. In order to save time and avoid chaos, we suggest you to look up classroom on our website before come to school.

  3. Textbook Purchase

    This year we will not be selling textbooks at the cafeteria; instead, the teacher of each Chinese class will bring the textbooks to his/her classroom. So please come to the classroom a little earlier and hand in the cash or check to the teacher to get the textbook. We would greatly appreciate if parent volunteers can help the teachers deliver the textbooks and collect payments.

  4. Information Session in Cafeteria

    Information tables will be set up in the cafeteria in B Wing to introduce our school and programs to new families. Our staff and volunteers can help to answer questions regarding school curriculum, PTA activities, and special programs, etc. Please stop by if you would like to know more about our school.

In addition, we would also like to draw your attention on the parking situation. The main parking lot in front of the Engleman Hall now has 30 more spaces available after the completion of the construction during the summer. But please be advised that DO NOT park in the reserved spaces for the President/Vice Presidents of the University and any other designated spaces with blue signs. If you park in those spaces, not only your car will be ticketed or towed, but also it will cause unnecessary trouble to our school. In addition to the main lot, Lots 5, 12, 12A, and 12B off Crescent Street near the Lyman Center, and the garage on Fitch Street are also available. Please see the attached map for the location of the parking lots and garage. Again, please keep in mind: no matter where you park, don’t park in the reserved spaces.

Meanwhile, we also want to update you on a new policy implemented by SCSU this summer. Starting August 25, 2015, SCSU is a TOBACCO-FREE CAMPUS, which means that ANY tobacco products on campus (including outdoor spaces and inside of personal vehicles) are prohibited. As the visitors of Southern, our members should respect and follow the University’s policy. For more information please visit: SouthernCT.edu/tobaccofree.

Thank you, and we really look forward to seeing you all this Sunday!

Best Regards,
Henan Cheng, Ed.D.
