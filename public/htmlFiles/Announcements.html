南康中文学校秋季新增文化课

Dear SCCS Members,

I am pleased to announce that the Southern Connecticut Chinese School (南康中文学校) will offer four additional enrichment classes during the fall semester in order to meet the diverse needs of our students:

  1. SSAT Preparation (Grades 6-7)
    Instructor: Mike Patrick
    Course Description:
    The SSAT class will familiarize students with the structure and content of the entrance exam most commonly used by private elementary and secondary schools. Students will complete practice exercises and mock tests. We will discuss strategies, study habits, and test-preparation skills. This class is designed for students in Grades 6 and 7. Time/Location: 1:20-2:05 P.M. /Room B211
    Tuition: $100/semester

  2. ACT Preparation (Middle/High School)
    Instructor: Mike Newcomer
    Course Description:
    The ACT is a standardized test that may be taken in place or in conjunction with the SAT for college admission. This course will cover material that is tested on the ACT and will teach students useful test-taking techniques in order to give them an edge on the test. Students will study from real ACT materials and will be given assignments to bolster the information learned in class. Time/Location: 1:00-2:00 P.M. /Room: C009
    Tuition: $130/semester

  3. Eli Whitney Science Workshop I & II (Grade 2 and up)



    Instructor: Sally Hill
    Course Description:
    The Eli Whitney Workshop is an experimental learning workshop for students between 7-14. We collect, interpret, and teach experiments that are the roots of design and invention. Please see the attached file for some sample projects.
    Time/Location: 3:10-3:55 P.M.; 4:05-4:50 P.M./Room: C140
    Tuition: $120/6 weeks, including material costs.

  4. Chinese pinyin (Grade 2 and up)
    Instructor: Ruhua Yang
    Course Description:

    Pinyin plays a very important role in Chinese language learning. It helps students pronounce correctly, and memorize the characters. The Chinese pinyin class is designed for students who are interested in improving their pinyin. We strongly encourage students Grade 2 and up who are struggling with pinyin to sign up the class.

    为什么要学好拼音?
    学好拼音是为了学会正确的发音,而正确的发音是中文学习的重要组成部分,对理解、记忆中文有很大的帮助. 同时, 在电子时代, 学好拼音,才能把中文变成电子文本, 快速地交流传递. 为了帮助低年级同学加强、巩固学过的拼音,中文学校计划在 2014年秋季增开拼音强化班, 欢迎 1年级以上的同学报名参加,课程将以强化拼音的听说读写练习为主.

    Time/Location: 4:05-4:50 P.M. /Room: B125
    Tuition: $50/6 weeks

尊敬的南康中文学校学生，家长和老师：

你们好！

时光飞逝，转眼之间难忘的2014年就要结束了。我谨代表中文学校全体老师和工作人员感谢大家在过去的一年里对中文学校的大力支持。我希望您和您的孩子在中文学校渡过了愉快，有意义的一年。

这个周日（12月14日）是中文学校在2014年的最后一天，在此有几件重要的事情需要提醒大家：

§ 首先，中文学校将于下午12点半至1点40分在C112小礼堂举办名为“欢歌诗语”的学生才艺表演。为了鼓励各年级的同学们的积极参与，学校特意将这个星期的所有课程顺延20分钟。第一节课将于1点40分开始上课，最后一节课将于5点10分下课。学校PTA稍后将会发出关于才艺表演的详细信息。

§ 其次，请各位家长将上周发给您的孩子的教学评估表于本周日交到A105 房间学校办公室。如果您尚未收到评估表，请下载附件中的表格。您的反馈和建议将会极大地帮助我们提高教学质量。

最后，再一次感谢您的信任与支持！祝您和家人新年快乐，健康幸福！
